Question: What is the primary function of money?

A: To serve as a means of cultural expression
B: To act as a medium of exchange
C: To create social hierarchies
D: To facilitate artistic endeavors
Correct Answer: B
Question: Which country is known for introducing paper money to the world?

A: Greece
B: India
C: China
D: Egypt
Correct Answer: C
Question: What term describes the decrease in the purchasing power of money, reflected in a general increase in prices?

A: Deflation
B: Inflation
C: Stagnation
D: Appreciation
Correct Answer: B
